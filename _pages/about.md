I am Mingjian Duan, a Ph.D. student at **Fudan University**, advised by **Prof. Weili Han**. My research focuses on **system and network security**, with particular interests in **password security**, **security of multi-agent systems**, and **zero-trust architectures**. I am broadly interested in building principled security mechanisms for emerging distributed and AI-driven systems.

Research Interests
======
- **Password Security**: data-driven password guessing models and the measurement and strengthening of password ecosystems
- **Multi-Agent System Security**: trust, isolation, and policy enforcement among autonomous (LLM-based) agents
- **Zero-Trust Architecture**: identity-centric, continuously verified security models for enterprise and cloud systems
